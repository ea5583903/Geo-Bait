Achievement Description
GB-84 Plus

GeoBait

Graph points into playable platform paths.

Point format: one x,y pair per line. Example: 6,10. Portal format: jet,12,3 or cube,20,1. Jump pad format: 18,2 or down,18,5. Pads bounce automatically. Jump orb format: 20,3 or down,20,6. Press Space while touching one. Blocks snap to a grid and overlapping or too-crooked lines are fixed or rejected. Start begins movement. Jump hops as cube, hold Jump/Space to fly as jet. Restart: R.
